Why Should You Grow?

Fun

It's a great way to have fun and try different types of chillies.

Variety

Adds different flavours to the food you make.

Easy

Growing chillies is easy to get into and maintain.

Cheap

Cheaper in the long run, saves money from buying store bought chillies.

What you need Getting Started Planting General Care

What you will need.

To begin with you will need some equipment (all are cheap and easy to find in any garden store or Amazon).

This includes:

  • A Propagator
  • Seeds
  • Zip lock bags
  • Potting soil
  • Biodegradable seedling pots
  • Plastic pots - 5 to 6inches

Getting Started.

You will first need to prepare your seeds, this is done by germination. Germination is the process of a seed growing shoots and is simple to do.

  1. You will need to get 2 pieces of kitchen roll.
  2. Soak a piece lightly with water
  3. Arrange your seeds in a grid on the piece
  4. Place the second piece on top.
  5. Fold the roll up and place inside the zip lock bag and leave on a warm surface for a couple of days(3-4 days).

When you notice tails coming out from your seeds you are then ready to move onto the next step and plant them.

Planting.

Now that your seeds have grown tails you are ready to plant them!
You will now need to prepare your seedling pots and this is quite simple. This is done by filling the pot with as much soil as you can then running some lukewarm water through the soil and pressing down gently to compact the soil(repeat this until the pot is full). Once your pots are ready make a small hole about 1cm deep and place 1 seed per pot and cover with soil.
The prepared pots can now be placed in a propagator which can be placed on a window sill so that the plants can get sunlight to help them grow.

General Care.

As your chillies grow you will need to adjust how you care for them, here are a few tips in order to get the best out of your plant.

  • As your plant grows larger be sure to repot into a larger plastic pot.
  • Water your plants only when the top of the soil feels dry.
  • Fertilizer is a great option if you want your plants to grow large and strong.
  • When your plant starts flowering it is recommend to place it outside for insects to pollinate.

Carolina Reaper

Previously known as the hottest chilli in the world, now passed by the Dragon's Breath chili and Pepper X. The Reaper has a sweet and fruity flavour, before the intense heat kicks in.
Not for the faint of heart but definitely for people looking for a challenge. Always keep a glass of milk in hand when eating.
